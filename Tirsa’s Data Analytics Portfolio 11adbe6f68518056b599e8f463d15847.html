  • Overview

    In this project, I analyzed bike sales data using Excel's Pivot Table to gain valuable insights into customer characteristics and sales trends. The dashboard is designed to help the company better understand their customers and optimize business strategies.

  • Scope

    Exploring bike sales data to uncover purchasing patterns based on demographic characteristics such as marital status, region, education level, average income, commute habits, and customer age brackets.

  • Tools:

    Excel (Pivot Table).

  • Methodology
    • üíæ Data Collection

      Used secondary dataset

      • Removed all duplicate records using the 'Remove Duplicates' function.
      • Renamed values in the 'Marital Status' attribute, changing 'M' to 'Married' and 'S' to 'Single.'
      • Renamed values in the 'Gender' attribute, changing 'F' to 'Female' and 'M' to 'Male.'
      • Created a new 'Age Bracket' attribute to categorize ages into 'Old,' 'Middle Age,' and 'Adolescent.'
    • üìà Pivot Table Analysis

      Used Pivot Tables to group and analyze data based on various customer demographic attributes.

    • üìä Visualization

      Created a visual dashboard displaying sales trends and purchasing patterns based on customer demographics.

  • üí° Insights & Recommendations
    • Average Income by Purchase

      Customers with higher average income are more likely to purchase bikes, indicating that income plays a significant role in purchasing decisions.

      Recommendation: Develop targeted marketing strategies for high-income individuals and promote premium bike models to this segment.

    • Count of Purchased Bikes by Customer Age Bracket

      Middle Age customers represent the highest purchase count, indicating a key demographic for targeting.

      Recommendation: Create targeted campaigns that focus on the benefits of biking for health and lifestyle specifically for the Middle Age demographic.

    • Count of Purchased Bikes by Customer Commute Distance

      Customers commuting within 0-1 miles show the highest purchase rates, suggesting targeted marketing in these areas.

      Recommendation: Focus advertising efforts on promoting bikes for short-distance commuting, highlighting convenience and efficiency.

    • Marital Status

      Selecting ‚ÄúMarried‚Äù showed a higher purchase rate, confirming that this demographic is more engaged.

      Recommendation: Increase marketing content aimed at married couples, especially wives, promoting cycling as a family-friendly activity.

    • Region

      Customers from North America have the highest average income per purchase compared to those in Europe and the Pacific region. The difference in average income between buyers and non-buyers is also significant in North America, indicating a strong purchasing potential. In Europe and the Pacific, the average incomes of buyers and non-buyers are closer together, suggesting a more challenging market.

      Recommendation: Adjust marketing strategy for North America to increase purchasing power, while developing targeted initiatives in Europe and the Pacific to encourage bicycle purchases.

    • Education Level

      Individuals with a high school education and partial college education have a higher purchase rate compared to those who did not purchase.

      Recommendation: Adjust marketing strategies to emphasize the health benefits and financial savings associated with cycling, targeting this educational demographic.

üì± "Smart Rinse" Mobile App

  • Overview

    Smart Rinse is a dual-purpose application designed for both end users and laundry business owners. The app for end users provides recommendations for nearby laundry services based on ratings and sentiment analysis of customer reviews, while the app for laundry business owners allows them to register their businesses and process orders online.

    As part of the machine learning team, I was responsible for developing the sentiment analysis model for laundry service reviews to enhance the recommendation system for end users. This project was recognized as part of the top 68 Capstone Project in Bangkit Academy 2023.

  • Scope

    To develop a machine learning model that performs sentiment analysis on laundry service reviews, categorizing them as positive or negative, and providing recommendations for end users based on review sentiment and proximity.

  • Tools & Technologies
    • Python (TensorFlow, Pandas, Numpy, Scikit-learn, NLTK)
    • Spreadsheet (for data labeling)
    • H5 Format (for model deployment)
  • Methodology:
    • Data Collection:

      We collected reviews of laundry services from Google Maps

    • Data Preparation & Cleaning:
      • Removed duplicates, irrelevant reviews, and cleaned up text data by eliminating special characters and performing tokenization.
      • Labeled reviews manually as positive or negative based on the overall sentiment conveyed by the customers.
    • Balancing the Dataset

      Ensured that the dataset was balanced between positive and negative reviews to avoid model bias.

    • Model Training & Validation
      • Trained the model using a balanced dataset and validated it with unseen data to ensure its effectiveness.
    • Model Development
      • Used TensorFlow to build and train the sentiment analysis model.
        Processed text data using techniques such as tokenization, padding, and embedding to prepare it for the model.
      • Evaluated different models to select the most accurate for the task.
